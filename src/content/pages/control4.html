Many Reasons to Use Control 4

Smart Operating System

A smart home operating system (OS) serves as the central hub, linking almost all the technological elements in your home. With the Control4 Smart Home OS 3, it becomes simple and enjoyable for you and your family to manage almost every gadget and system within your residence.

Smart Lighting

Control4 Smart Lighting enables you to switch all the lights in your home on or off as you depart or arrive, instantly initiate a playlist in every room, get alerts about unlocked doors needing attention, and experience self-adjusting lights ensuring you never return to a dark home.

Security

Control4 Connect keeps you informed about home activities when you're not there, ensuring peace of mind about your children's safe return. It offers the convenience of remotely managing cameras, door locks, lighting, and other features. Additionally, it provides voice control capabilities and equips you with customization options to enhance your smart home experience post-installation.
