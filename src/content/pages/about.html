About Army Electric

At Army Electric, we've been illuminating Toronto's commercial, residential, and industrial spaces with dedication and expertise for over a decade. From the heart of the city's bustling commercial districts to the cozy neighborhoods that make Toronto home, we bring a personal touch to every job, ensuring safety, efficiency, and innovation.

Driven by a deep understanding of our clients' needs, we tailor our solutions to bring their visions to life, combining technical expertise with a human touch. We believe in building relationships that last, grounded in trust, transparency, and mutual respect.

We started Army Electric with the belief that a good electrician doesn't just fix problems but lights up lives. That's the spark that drives us every day.

Michael Hunter CEO-Founder
